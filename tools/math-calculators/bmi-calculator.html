About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's calculated using the formula:

BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)Â²

BMI is a useful screening tool but doesn't directly measure body fat or account for muscle mass, bone density, or overall body composition.

BMI Categories
  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
Important Notes
  • BMI may not be accurate for athletes with high muscle mass
  • Different BMI ranges may apply to different ethnic groups
  • BMI is not suitable for pregnant women
  • Consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice
  • BMI is just one indicator of health
